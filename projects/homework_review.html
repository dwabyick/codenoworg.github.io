Homework Overview

In between sessions 2 and 3 students complete lessons 1-6 of the Ruby track on Codecademy.com. These exercises help the students review the Ruby and programming concepts they are introduced to during the workshop.

Specifically, the lessons cover the following concepts:

Lessons 1 and 2: Introduction to Ruby and Putting the Form in Formatter

  1. Overview of Ruby as a programming language
  2. Data types: numbers, strings, and booleans
  3. Declaring and setting variables †
  4. Math operations: +, –, *, /, **, and %
  5. String methods: .length, .reverse, .upcase, .downcase, .split
  6. Output: puts and print
  7. Writing comments
  8. Naming conventions †

Lessons 3 and 4: Control Flow in Ruby and Thith Meanth War!

  1. Boolean operations: And, Or, Not, and combinations
  2. Control flow: If, Else, Elsif, Unless †
  3. Comparisons: Equal or Not?, Less Than or Greater Than

Lessons 5 and 6: Loops & Iterators and Redacted!

  1. Data structures: arrays
  2. Loops: while/end, until/end; for loop and ranges †
  3. Iterators: loop, .times, and .each (using it with arrays)

†: Indicates concepts many students find challenging
